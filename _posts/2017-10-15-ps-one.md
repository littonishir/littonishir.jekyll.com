![](/img/in-post/ps-one/ps-20171017152129.jpg)
 
### 1.2 将要P的图拖进PS

![](/img/in-post/ps-one/ps-20171017152249.jpg)
 
### 1.3 按住shift鼠标拖动图片(图片原比例放大缩小)

![](/img/in-post/ps-one/ps-20171017152301.jpg)

![](/img/in-post/ps-one/ps-20171017152334.jpg)
